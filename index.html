Who We Are

A Multifaceted
Enterprise Built

FORME LIMITED is a diversified holding company operating at the intersection of education, technology, and energy. We believe that lasting progress comes from building strong institutions — schools that form great minds, tech hubs that forge great products, and energy infrastructure that fuels great nations.

From our classrooms to our server rooms and beyond, every arm of FORME LIMITED is united by a singular mission: to deliver excellence that transforms communities and industries alike.
